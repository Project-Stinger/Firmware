Personalize your Stinger ML idle

Record one session, plug in, and we’ll train both models (LR + MLP), show you what they’re predicting, then load your custom weights without reflashing.

Web Serial (Chrome/Edge) No UF2 for model upload LR + MLP

1) Update firmware

  1. Flash the latest V2 firmware: stinger-v2-latest.uf2.
  2. Verify in the Device menu you see: Start ML Recording / Stop ML Recording.

2) Record a session (about 15 minutes max)

The logger stores IMU + trigger at 100Hz in onboard flash. You have about 14–15 minutes per session. Record one continuous session that includes your normal aiming style.

  • Play normally: aim, track targets, move around like you actually do.
  • Include negatives: spend some time aiming/moving without firing. This teaches the model what “not about to shoot” looks like.
  • Include positives: do a bunch of real shots throughout the session.
  • Try to avoid rapid double-taps; shots <1s apart may be marked invalid to avoid recoil spillover.
Menu → Device → Start ML Recording … play … Menu → Device → Stop ML Recording

3) Plug in

  1. Remove the top panel with the Picatinny rail to expose the USB port.
  2. Plug the blaster into your computer via USB.
  3. Keep it plugged in until the end (we’ll upload your model over the same connection).
